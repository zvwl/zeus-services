## FAQ

**Q: When will I see traffic?**
A: Impressions start appearing in 3-7 days. Actual clicks usually start within 1-2 weeks. Significant traffic takes 4-8 weeks as pages move up rankings.

**Q: Can I speed up ranking?**
A: Quality backlinks are the main external ranking factor. On-page (done ✓) + backlinks = ranking success.

**Q: Should I build backlinks now?**
A: Get indexed first (1-2 weeks). Monitor rankings for 2-3 weeks. Then build 1-2 quality backlinks per week.

**Q: How many pages until I rank?**
A: You're set for ranking. Now it's time (indexing) + quality signals (backlinks, engagement) = rankings.

**Q: What if nothing ranks?**
A: Most likely causes: (1) Pages not indexed (check GSC), (2) Competition too high (target easier keywords), (3) No external links (build 3-5 quality backlinks).
