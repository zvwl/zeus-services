## ⚠️ Important Warnings

### DON'T Do These (Black Hat SEO)
- ❌ Keyword stuffing (repeating keyword 10+ times)
- ❌ Hidden text (white text on white background)
- ❌ Cloaking (showing different content to Google vs users)
- ❌ Private link networks (PBN)
- ❌ Paid links from low-quality sites
- ❌ Auto-generated content
- ❌ Doorway pages
- ❌ Duplicating content across pages

### DO These (White Hat SEO)
- ✅ Write unique, valuable content
- ✅ Natural keyword usage
- ✅ Build genuine backlinks
- ✅ Improve user experience
- ✅ Mobile optimization
- ✅ Fast page speed
- ✅ Regular content updates
- ✅ Transparent practices
