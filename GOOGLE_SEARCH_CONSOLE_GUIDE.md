## 📊 Key Reports to Watch

### Coverage Report
**What to look for:**
- ✅ ALL = Indexed pages (should grow)
- ⚠️ WARNING = Pages discoverable but not indexed yet
- ❌ ERROR = Pages with crawl problems

**Target**: 20+ indexed pages within 4 weeks

### Performance Report
**Key Metrics:**
- **Total Clicks**: How many people clicked from search
- **Impressions**: How many times you appeared in results
- **CTR**: Click-Through Rate (good target: 3-5%)
- **Avg Position**: Your ranking (lower = better)

**Action**: 
- Click on queries with high impressions but low CTR
- Improve titles/descriptions to increase CTR

### Core Web Vitals
**What Google Measures:**
1. **LCP** (Largest Contentful Paint) - Page load speed
   - Good: < 2.5 seconds
   - Your site: Very fast (prerendered)

2. **FID** (First Input Delay) - Responsiveness
   - Good: < 100ms
   - Your site: Excellent

3. **CLS** (Cumulative Layout Shift) - Stability
   - Good: < 0.1
   - Your site: Stable

## 🚨 Error Handling

### If you see "Crawl errors":

1. **Server error (5xx)**
   - Your server is down
   - Contact hosting provider

2. **Not found (404)**
   - Page doesn't exist
   - Remove from sitemap or fix URL

3. **Robots.txt issue**
   - robots.txt blocking crawl
   - Check `public/robots.txt`
   - Should allow `/`

### If pages aren't indexing:
1. Check Coverage report for errors
2. Use "Request indexing" for each page
3. Check robots.txt (shouldn't block)
4. Verify canonical URLs are correct
5. Wait 1-2 weeks for re-crawl
